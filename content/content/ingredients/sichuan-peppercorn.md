## 🌶️ What It Is
Dried husks of prickly ash berries.

## 🔥 What It’s Good In
- Mapo tofu
- Chili oil

## 📜 History
Sichuan pepper has been used in China for culinary and medicinal purposes for centuries.

## 🔧 How to Use It
Toast lightly, grind.

## 🔁 Substitutions
None
