## What It Is
Maggi is a concentrated seasoning sauce first developed in Switzerland in the late 1800s. Regional versions vary in flavor and salt level.

## What It Is Good In
- Noodles
- Stir-fries
- Soups
- Egg dishes
- Marinades

## 📜 History
Maggi began in Switzerland in 1886, when Julius Maggi produced the seasoning industrially.

## How to Use It
Use small amounts first (about 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon), taste, then add more as needed.

## Flavor Notes
Salty, dark, and savory with strong umami. It works best as a supporting seasoning, not the main flavor.

## Substitutions
- Golden Mountain sauce
- Light soy sauce plus a pinch of sugar (for balance)
