## 🍄 What It Is
Dried shiitakes concentrate flavor.

## 🍲 What It’s Good In
- Braises
- Soups
- Dumpling fillings

## 📜 History
Shiitake are native to East Asia, and the earliest written record of cultivation dates to 1209 in China.

## 🔧 How to Use It
Soak, reserve soaking liquid.

## 🔁 Substitutions
Fresh shiitake (lighter)
