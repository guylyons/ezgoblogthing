## ✨ What It Is
Monosodium glutamate enhances savoriness.

## 🍜 What It’s Good In
- Soups
- Stir-fries

## 📜 History
MSG was first prepared in 1908, when Kikunae Ikeda isolated the savory taste of kombu in Japan.

## 🔧 How to Use It
Pinch at end of cooking.

## 🔁 Substitutions
None (salt lacks umami)
