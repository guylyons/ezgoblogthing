## Length

| From | To | Formula |
|---|---|---|
| Millimeters (mm) | Centimeters (cm) | `cm = mm / 10` |
| Centimeters (cm) | Meters (m) | `m = cm / 100` |
| Meters (m) | Kilometers (km) | `km = m / 1000` |
| Kilometers (km) | Meters (m) | `m = km * 1000` |

## Mass

| From | To | Formula |
|---|---|---|
| Milligrams (mg) | Grams (g) | `g = mg / 1000` |
| Grams (g) | Kilograms (kg) | `kg = g / 1000` |
| Kilograms (kg) | Metric tons (t) | `t = kg / 1000` |

## Volume

| From | To | Formula |
|---|---|---|
| Milliliters (mL) | Liters (L) | `L = mL / 1000` |
| Liters (L) | Cubic meters (m^3) | `m^3 = L / 1000` |

## Temperature

| From | To | Formula |
|---|---|---|
| Celsius (C) | Kelvin (K) | `K = C + 273.15` |
| Kelvin (K) | Celsius (C) | `C = K - 273.15` |

## Area

| From | To | Formula |
|---|---|---|
| Square centimeters (cm^2) | Square meters (m^2) | `m^2 = cm^2 / 10000` |
| Square meters (m^2) | Hectares (ha) | `ha = m^2 / 10000` |
| Hectares (ha) | Square kilometers (km^2) | `km^2 = ha / 100` |
