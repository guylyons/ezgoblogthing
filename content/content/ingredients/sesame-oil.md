## 🌰 What It Is
Toasted sesame oil is made from roasted sesame seeds and is used for aroma, not frying.

## 🍳 What It’s Good In
- Stir-fries (finish)
- Noodles
- Dumpling fillings

## 📜 History
Sesame oil is one of the earliest-known crop-based oils.

## 🔧 How to Use It
Use sparingly at the end.

## 🔁 Substitutions
None — unique flavor
