## 🫘 What It Is
Fermented and dried black soybeans.

## 🍳 What It’s Good In
- Fish
- Stir-fries

## 📜 History
Douchi is one of the oldest known soy foods, with examples found in a Han tomb from around 165 BCE.

## 🔧 How to Use It
Rinse lightly before use.

## 🔁 Substitutions
Black bean garlic sauce
