## 🧊 What It Is
Crystallized cane sugar that dissolves slowly.

## 🍖 What It’s Good In
- Red braises
- Sauces

## 📜 History
Sugar candy has ancient roots in India and China, where sugar production spread over time.

## 🔧 How to Use It
Melt gently before adding liquid.

## 🔁 Substitutions
White sugar (less depth)
